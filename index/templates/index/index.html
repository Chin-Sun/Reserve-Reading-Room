Welcome to our website!

This is the Concordia Textbook Sharing System. We have developed a unique textbook-sharing model specifically for exam season. Our librarian noticed an increasing demand for course textbooks and proposed a solution to share them with a group of students in the library reading room. Join our community of Concordia students and share textbooks, study together, and succeed together!

Learn More

Feature 1

Students can check for available reading rooms by course number or textbook. And they can reserve a room and order a textbook if needed. One room per course per time slot.

Feature 2

The system allows users to book the room for a maximum of 5 hours. The maximum number of reading rooms for one course will be determined by the number of registered students for that course.

Feature 3

The course instructor has access to the booking logs and can see which students are studying together.
